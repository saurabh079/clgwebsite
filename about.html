About College

S.S.P.M. College of Engineering, Sindhudurg, Maharashtra was established in the year 1999, with the aim of making professional education available to the masses in rural Konkan region. The institute is affiliated to University of Mumbai and approved by AICTE. S.S.P.M College of Engineering has also been recently accredited by NAAC with a B+ grade. The institute offers undergraduate programs in streams like Computer, Electrical, Electronics and Telecommunication and Mechanical. The institute offers students the facilities of a Training and Placement Cell. All on-campus students are eligible to take benefit of this facility.
